CFD Trading

Winstone Prime offers CFD Trading or “Contract For Difference” trading. CFD is a complex financial product consisting of a contractual agreement between a buyer and a seller concerning changes in the price of an asset without transfer of asset.

The underlying asset can be a currency, a commodity, a company stock or even a sector or a global index. When the price of the asset differs from the price stated in the contract, the buyer or the seller of the CFD makes a profit or a loss accordingly. If the price goes up, the buyer profits and the seller loses, and vice versa.

CFD Trading can be used for -

  • Tax saving
  • Hedging or disguising a position
  • Acquiring controlling stakes in stock of a company

Winatone prime is committed to confidential and safe CFD Trading for our clients. Our investors are advised to understand the risks associated with CFD trading before embarking on it.
